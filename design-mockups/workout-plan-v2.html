How it works

Three steps. Then it just runs.

01

Tell Kip about you

Goal, age, how many days you can run, where you live. Three minutes. No watch needed.

02

Get your two weeks

A real plan — easy days, hard days, a long one. Each run with a warm-up and cool-down written out.

03

Tell it how each run felt

A number from 1 to 10, after the run. Sunday night, Kip rewrites the next two weeks based on what you said.
