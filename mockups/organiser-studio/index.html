Refund policy & T&Cs

Both are markdown. Runners agree to these at checkout. Both required to publish.

Refund policy

Refunds

  • Full refund if you cancel before Jun 5, 2026 (10 days before the run window opens).
  • 50% refund until Jun 10, 2026.
  • No refund after Jun 10 — your medal still ships if you submit a valid run.

If we cancel the event for any reason, we refund 100% automatically within 7 working days.

Contact hello@pacingproject.in to request a cancellation.

System refund rules

Terms & conditions

Terms

By registering you agree to:

  • Run the full distance you registered for within the run window.
  • Submit a single valid screenshot from Strava, Garmin, Apple Health, or Google Fit.
  • We may reject submissions that look edited, look like indoor treadmill runs, or that don't match your registered distance within ±2%.
  • Medals are shipped only to verified finishers, to the address you entered at checkout.
