⑧ FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is it really free?
Yes. There's no fee for clubs to list, host runs, or manage members. We earn from event ticketing and partners.
What does hosting an event cost?
Hosting is free. For ticketed events, standard payment processing applies — and we run free Instagram/Google ads to help fill it.
Do I have to share my phone number?
No. Members reach you through in-app chat. Your number stays private.
How do members find my club?
Your page ranks on Google, shows in the in-app discover feed, and gets featured in city roundups.
Can you set the page up for me?
Yes — our concierge option imports your last runs, writes the first recap, and hands you the keys. Free, limited slots.
What kinds of clubs is this for?
Any run club in India — training groups, weekend crews, beginner pods, women-only clubs.
